Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Resume Writer work?

When editing a resume, click the **AI Writer** button next to Summary or Experience. Enter your job role and keywords, then click Generate. The AI will draft high-impact statements that you can instantly insert into your fields.

How do I download a PDF of my resume?

Simply click on the **Download PDF** button in the header of the resume editor. The PDF will be created dynamically and saved in your computer's Downloads folder.

What is Profile Completeness?

Profile Completeness measures how comprehensive your default resume is. Complete all five steps (Basics, Work History, Education, Skills, and Projects) to achieve a 100% score and ensure your resume is fully optimized for ATS filters.
